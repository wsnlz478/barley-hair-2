Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What makes contacting Barley easy and trustworthy for international patients? +
Barley offers multiple easy contact options including WeChat, WhatsApp, and email with 24/7 English-speaking support, dedicated international patient coordinators, and transparent, comprehensive service from your first message through your final results.
Why should international patients feel comfortable contacting Barley? +
Barley has helped thousands of international patients with dedicated English-speaking support, transparent information, no-pressure consultations, and a proven track record making international patients feel completely comfortable.
How can I contact Barley as an international patient? +
International patients can contact Barley via WeChat (15810155700), WhatsApp (+86 15810155700), or email (hairtransplant@qq.com). Our team responds quickly in English to answer all your questions.
What information do I need for my first contact with Barley? +
For your first contact, simply share your hair loss concerns, goals, and any photos you're comfortable sharing. We'll guide you through the next steps and answer all your questions completely.
Is there someone available 24/7 if I need to contact Barley? +
Yes! Barley provides 24/7 support for international patients. Our dedicated team is available around the clock to answer questions, address concerns, and provide assistance whenever you need it.
Can I get a free consultation before contacting Barley? +
Absolutely! All consultations with Barley are completely free with no obligation. We'll discuss your goals, evaluate your situation, and provide a personalized treatment plan and transparent pricing information.
What clinic locations can I contact Barley about? +
You can contact Barley about any of our 30+ clinics across China including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, and many other major cities. We'll help you choose the best location.
Will the same person help me from first contact through treatment? +
Yes! You'll have a dedicated international patient coordinator who will help you from your first contact through your procedure and follow-up care for a seamless, consistent experience.
How quickly does Barley respond to international patient inquiries? +
Barley's international patient team typically responds within 1-2 hours during business hours and always within 24 hours. We understand the importance of timely communication for our international patients.
Is contacting Barley completely free with no obligation? +
Absolutely! There's no cost or obligation to contact Barley. All consultations and information are completely free, and you can decide at any time whether to proceed with treatment.
