Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What Norwood scales of baldness can be treated? +
We successfully treat Norwood 2 through Norwood 7, including frontal recession, crown baldness, and complete scalp baldness. Our expert doctors in China will assess your specific Norwood classification and create a personalized restoration plan.
Can severe baldness be restored in one session? +
Yes! Our advanced Chinese techniques and high-density transplantation allow us to address even Norwood 6-7 baldness in a single session in many cases. During your consultation at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics, we'll assess your donor supply and determine the best approach.
How many grafts are needed for severe baldness? +
Severe baldness restoration typically requires 2,500-5,000+ grafts depending on the Norwood scale and desired coverage. Our Chinese medical team maximizes every graft using our patented microneedle technology for optimal density and coverage.
Will the transplanted hair look natural on a bald scalp? +
Absolutely! We create natural-looking hairlines with irregular placement, single-follicle fronts, and gradual density transitions. Our artistic approach ensures results that look completely authentic, even on completely bald scalps.
What if I have limited donor hair for severe baldness? +
Our expert doctors in China specialize in maximizing limited donor supply. We can use body hair (beard, chest) as supplementary donor sources in some cases, and strategically place available follicles for optimal coverage and natural appearance.
Can I combine medical therapy with baldness restoration? +
Yes! We recommend combining transplantation with medical therapy (Minoxidil, Finasteride, PRP) to stabilize existing hair loss and maximize results. This comprehensive approach ensures your restoration looks great for years to come.
How long does a severe baldness procedure take? +
Comprehensive baldness restoration typically takes 6-10 hours depending on graft count. With our 30+ clinics across China, you can complete even extensive procedures in a single day with our experienced medical teams.
When will I see full results from baldness restoration? +
Transplanted hairs shed in the first 2-3 weeks (normal!). New growth begins at 3-4 months, with significant coverage at 6 months. Full, mature results are typically visible at 12-18 months as the transplanted hairs thicken and mature.
Can crown baldness be treated separately? +
Absolutely! Crown restoration requires special attention to the natural whorl pattern and hair direction. Our Chinese doctors are specially trained in crown techniques, creating natural-looking density that follows your existing growth pattern perfectly.
Is there anything I should do before baldness restoration? +
We recommend starting medical therapy 1-3 months before surgery to stabilize hair loss, avoiding blood thinners, and staying healthy. Our team will provide comprehensive pre-procedure instructions during your consultation at any of our Chinese clinics.
