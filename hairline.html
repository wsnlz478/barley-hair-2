Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How is the hairline design personalized for each patient? +
Our expert doctors analyze your face shape, facial features, age, and personal preferences to create a customized hairline that looks completely natural. We consider facial symmetry, proportions, and your unique style to design a hairline that complements your appearance perfectly.
What makes a hairline look natural? +
A natural hairline has an irregular, slightly curved shape rather than being perfectly straight. We use single follicles at the front for softness, gradually increasing density towards the back, and plant hairs at natural angles following your existing growth pattern.
Can I choose my hairline shape? +
Absolutely! We work with you to understand your preferences and lifestyle. Our artistic team will present several design options based on your facial analysis, and together we'll select the perfect hairline that matches your vision while looking completely natural.
How many grafts are typically needed for a hairline transplant? +
Hairline transplantation usually requires 1,000-2,500 grafts depending on the degree of recession and desired density. During your free consultation at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics, we'll assess your specific needs and provide an exact graft count recommendation.
Can women have hairline transplantation? +
Yes! Hairline restoration is equally popular among women. We understand the unique needs of female patients and create softer, more feminine hairlines that enhance facial beauty while maintaining a completely natural appearance.
Will the transplanted hairline look fake? +
No! Our patented micro-needle technology and artistic approach ensure completely natural results. We use irregular placement, single-follicle front lines, and natural directional planting. Our 18+ years of experience in China have perfected the art of creating undetectable hairlines.
How long does it take for the hairline to grow in naturally? +
Transplanted hairs typically shed in the first 2-3 weeks (this is normal!). New growth begins at 3-4 months, and you'll see significant improvement by 6 months. The final, natural-looking hairline is fully visible at 9-12 months.
Can I have a hairline transplant without shaving my head? +
Yes! Our no-shave technique is perfect for hairline restoration, especially for professionals who want discreet treatment. You can keep your existing hair while we transplant the new hairline, and no one will notice you had a procedure.
What makes Barley different from other hair transplant clinics? +
Barley is China's first and largest specialized hair transplant hospital chain with 18+ years of experience. Our patented micro-needle technology, developed in China, allows for 95-98% follicle survival rates. With 30+ clinics across China and an artistic team specialized in hairline design, we've helped thousands of international patients achieve natural, permanent results.
How much does a hairline transplant cost at Barley? +
Hairline transplant costs at Barley depend on the number of grafts needed, typically ranging from 1,000-2,500 grafts. We offer competitive pricing compared to Western clinics while maintaining world-class quality. During your free consultation, we'll provide a detailed, personalized quote based on your specific needs and desired results.
Is the hairline transplant permanent? +
Absolutely! The transplanted hairs are taken from the donor area (back of scalp) which is genetically resistant to DHT. These hairs will grow permanently and naturally for a lifetime, maintaining the same characteristics as your original hair.
Can I see before and after photos of Barley's hairline patients? +
Yes! We have an extensive gallery of real patient results showing natural hairline transformations from our clinics across China. During your consultation, we can show you specific examples similar to your hair type and desired results. Our success cases demonstrate the artistic excellence and natural outcomes Barley is known for worldwide.
