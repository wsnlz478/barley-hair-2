Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What is FUE hair transplant and how does it work at Barley? +
FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) is a minimally invasive hair transplant technique where individual follicular units are extracted directly from the donor area using micro-punches. At Barley, we combine FUE with our patented microneedle implantation technology for superior results with minimal trauma and faster recovery.
How much does FUE hair transplant cost compared to Western clinics? +
FUE at Barley costs 50-70% less than comparable procedures in the US, UK, or Australia. While Western clinics typically charge $15,000-$25,000, Barley's FUE procedures range from $3,000-$8,000 depending on graft count, with the same or better quality using our patented Chinese technology.
What size punches does Barley use for FUE extraction? +
Barley uses precision punches ranging from 0.6mm to 1.0mm for FUE extraction, carefully selected based on your individual hair characteristics. Our smaller punch sizes minimize trauma, reduce scarring, and preserve more donor tissue for future procedures if needed.
What does the FUE donor area scarring look like at Barley? +
With Barley's advanced FUE technique, the donor area heals with tiny, pinpoint scars that are virtually undetectable when your hair is grown out. Our precision extraction method minimizes trauma and allows for quicker healing compared to traditional FUE methods.
What makes Barley's FUE technique different from other clinics? +
Barley combines 18+ years of specialized FUE experience with our patented Chinese microneedle implantation technology. With 30+ clinics across China, we've performed over 500,000 successful procedures, making us China's largest and most trusted hair transplant hospital chain.
How soon can I return to work after FUE at Barley? +
Most patients can safely return to desk work 2-3 days after FUE at Barley. Our minimal-trauma technique means less swelling and faster recovery. Many of our international patients combine the procedure with a short vacation in China and return to work looking completely natural.
Can I have FUE if I've had previous hair transplant procedures? +
Yes! Barley's FUE technique is excellent for revisional hair transplant surgery. Our expert doctors can carefully work around existing scars, extract remaining healthy donor hair, and use our microneedle technology to fill in gaps or correct previous results for a natural appearance.
Does Barley offer international patient support for FUE procedures? +
Absolutely! Barley has a dedicated international patient team that assists with everything from initial consultation to post-op care. With 30+ clinics across major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, we make your FUE journey to China seamless and comfortable.
