Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What makes Barley's medical team unique among hair transplant clinics? +
Barley's doctors have 10+ years average experience, international training backgrounds, and have performed over 1 million procedures collectively. Our team includes specialists for both men and women with specialized expertise.
Why should international patients trust Barley's medical team? +
Barley's doctors have extensive experience treating international patients, English-speaking capabilities, and understand diverse aesthetic preferences from around the world to create natural results that suit each patient.
How experienced are Barley's hair transplant doctors? +
Barley's doctors average 10+ years of specialized experience in hair restoration, with many having international training and certifications. Collectively, our medical team has performed over 1 million successful procedures.
Does Barley have specialized doctors for women? +
Yes! Barley has female specialists who understand the unique needs of women's hair restoration, including eyebrow transplantation, hairline refinement, and female pattern hair loss treatment.
Will I meet my doctor before the procedure at Barley? +
Absolutely! During your free consultation, you'll meet with your doctor who will evaluate your condition, discuss your goals, explain treatment options, and create a personalized plan just for you.
What training do Barley's doctors receive? +
Barley's doctors complete rigorous training including our proprietary techniques, ongoing professional development, and mastery of our patented technologies to ensure consistent, excellent results for every patient.
Can Barley's doctors handle revision cases? +
Yes! Our experienced doctors regularly handle revision and touch-up cases, whether your original procedure was at Barley or elsewhere. We'll evaluate and recommend the best approach for optimal results.
Will the same doctor perform my entire procedure at Barley? +
Yes! Your primary doctor will oversee and perform your entire procedure from start to finish, ensuring consistency and quality throughout with the process for the best possible results.
How does Barley ensure the quality of its doctors across all 30+ clinics? +
Barley has strict hiring standards, centralized training programs, regular quality audits, and the same certification requirements across all clinics to ensure consistent quality and expertise everywhere in China.
Can Barley's doctors communicate effectively with international patients? +
Absolutely! Barley has English-speaking doctors and dedicated translators available. Our medical team understands cultural nuances and aesthetic preferences from around the world to deliver natural results that suit each patient.
