Comprehensive Services Across China

At Barley, China's first and largest specialized hair transplant hospital chain, we offer a complete range of hair restoration services across our 30+ clinics in major Chinese cities. Whether you visit our Beijing headquarters, Shanghai flagship clinic, or any of our other locations in Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, or beyond, you'll receive the same world-class quality and personalized care.

From hairline transplantation and hair thickening to eyebrow and beard restoration, our expert medical team in China creates personalized treatment plans that consider your cultural preferences, lifestyle, and aesthetic goals. Every service at Barley is backed by our patented microneedle technology and 18+ years of experience pioneering hair restoration in China.

International patients choosing Barley for their hair restoration journey benefit from our full-service support including travel arrangements, translation services, and dedicated aftercare - all while experiencing the expertise that has made us China's most trusted name in hair transplantation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What makes Barley's service options unique among hair transplant clinics? +
Barley offers the most comprehensive range of hair restoration services in China with 30+ clinics, 10+ patented technologies, and specialized services for every hair restoration need including hairline, thickening, eyebrows, beard, and medical treatment.
Why should international patients choose Barley for comprehensive services? +
International patients choose Barley for our complete one-stop service experience including airport pickup, translation, hotel arrangements, follow-up care, and our commitment to excellence at 50-70% savings compared to Western countries.
What hair restoration services does Barley offer? +
Barley offers a comprehensive range of hair restoration services including hairline transplantation, hair thickening, baldness restoration, eyebrow transplantation, beard transplantation, and medical hair loss treatment.
Can I combine multiple services in one visit to Barley? +
Absolutely! Many patients at Barley choose to combine services for comprehensive results. Common combinations include hairline restoration with crown thickening, or beard transplantation with medical therapy.
Does Barley offer services for women as well? +
Yes! Barley provides specialized hair restoration services for women, including female pattern hair loss treatment, eyebrow transplantation, hairline refinement, and hair thickening.
What's included in Barley's comprehensive service package? +
Barley's service packages include pre-procedure consultation, the procedure itself, all post-operative medications, follow-up appointments, and comprehensive aftercare instructions.
Does Barley offer touch-up or revision services? +
Yes! Barley offers touch-up and revision services for patients who want additional density or refinement. Our expert doctors can evaluate your situation and recommend the best approach.
What aftercare services does Barley provide? +
Barley provides comprehensive aftercare including detailed written instructions, follow-up appointments, and 24/7 access to our medical team if you have questions or concerns during recovery.
How does Barley ensure consistent quality across all 30+ clinics in China? +
Barley maintains strict quality control through standardized training, centralized quality management systems, regular clinic audits, and the same patented technologies and protocols across all locations.
Can I choose which Barley clinic in China to visit for my procedure? +
Absolutely! You can choose any of Barley's 30+ clinics in major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, and more. Our international patient team can help you select the best location.
