Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What is no-shave hair transplant and how does it work at Barley? +
No-shave hair transplant at Barley is an advanced technique where we strategically harvest and implant grafts without shaving your entire head. Our expert team carefully works around your existing hair, making the procedure virtually undetectable even during recovery. This is perfect for professionals or anyone who wants complete discretion.
Can I really have a hair transplant without anyone noticing at Barley? +
Absolutely! Barley's no-shave technique allows you to maintain your normal hairstyle throughout the entire process. We strategically extract from concealed areas and implant in a way that blends seamlessly with your existing hair. Many of our international patients return to work or social activities without anyone knowing they had a procedure.
Is no-shave hair transplant suitable for all cases at Barley? +
Barley's no-shave technique is suitable for most patients, particularly those needing mild to moderate restoration. During your consultation, our expert doctors will assess if no-shave is right for you, or if we might recommend a hybrid approach for more extensive cases while still maintaining maximum discretion.
How does Barley extract grafts without shaving the donor area? +
At Barley, we use specialized techniques and precision instruments to extract grafts from strategic sections of the donor area while leaving surrounding hair intact. Our skilled technicians carefully part and isolate small sections for extraction, allowing your longer hair to naturally cover the tiny extraction sites immediately after the procedure.
Can women have no-shave hair transplantation at Barley? +
Yes! No-shave hair transplant is particularly popular with our female patients at Barley. Many women prefer not to shave their hair for social or professional reasons, and our no-shave technique allows them to undergo hair restoration with complete privacy and discretion throughout the entire process.
What is recovery like after no-shave hair transplant at Barley? +
Recovery after Barley's no-shave procedure is typically even more comfortable because you maintain your normal hairstyle. The tiny extraction sites heal quickly and are naturally covered by your existing hair. Most patients feel comfortable returning to public activities within days, with no visible signs of having had a procedure.
Can I have no-shave hair transplant combined with microneedle technology at Barley? +
Absolutely! In fact, Barley's no-shave technique pairs beautifully with our patented microneedle implantation. The precision of our microneedle technology allows us to place grafts more precisely between existing hairs, creating even more natural and seamless results while maintaining complete discretion throughout the process.
How soon can I get a haircut after no-shave transplant at Barley? +
We recommend waiting about 2-3 weeks before having a professional haircut after your no-shave procedure at Barley. You can gently wash and style your existing hair as normal after the first few days, but we suggest avoiding clippers or aggressive cutting near the transplant sites until they're fully healed.
