Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What is FUT hair transplant and how does it work at Barley? +
FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation), also known as strip harvesting, is a hair transplant technique where a thin strip of scalp is removed from the donor area. At Barley, we then use our patented microneedle technology to implant the individual follicular units for superior results. FUT is particularly effective for patients needing large numbers of grafts.
How much does FUT cost compared to Western clinics? +
FUT at Barley costs 50-70% less than comparable procedures in the US, UK, or Australia. While Western clinics typically charge $15,000-$30,000 for large cases, Barley's FUT procedures offer the same or better quality at a fraction of the price, with our 18+ years of specialized Chinese expertise.
How many grafts can typically be harvested with FUT at Barley? +
Barley's skilled surgical team can typically harvest 3,000-5,000 grafts in a single FUT session, sometimes more depending on your individual donor characteristics. This makes FUT an excellent choice for patients with extensive baldness who need maximum grafts in a single procedure.
What makes Barley's FUT technique different from other clinics? +
Barley combines 18+ years of specialized FUT experience with our patented Chinese microneedle implantation and advanced trichophytic closure techniques. With 30+ clinics across China and over 500,000 successful procedures, we're China's largest and most trusted hair transplant hospital, offering unmatched expertise in FUT.
What does the FUT scar look like and can it be hidden? +
With Barley's advanced FUT technique and meticulous closure methods, the linear scar is typically very thin - often just 1-2mm wide. When your hair is grown out even slightly, the scar is virtually undetectable. Many patients continue to wear shorter hairstyles without any visible evidence of the procedure.
How soon can I return to work after FUT at Barley? +
Most FUT patients at Barley can return to desk work within 3-5 days. The donor area sutures or staples are typically removed at 10-14 days. Many of our international patients combine their FUT procedure with a short vacation in China and return to work looking completely natural with fantastic density.
Can FUT be combined with microneedle implantation at Barley? +
Absolutely! At Barley, we often combine FUT strip harvesting with our patented microneedle implantation technology. This hybrid approach gives you the graft harvesting efficiency of FUT with the superior implantation precision and results of our microneedle technology for the best of both techniques.
Does Barley offer international patient support for FUT procedures? +
Absolutely! Barley has a dedicated international patient team that assists with everything from initial consultation to post-op care. With 30+ clinics across major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, we make your FUT journey to China seamless and comfortable from start to finish.
