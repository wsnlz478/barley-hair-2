Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What post-transplant care products does Barley recommend? +
Barley offers medical-grade shampoos, conditioners, and serums specifically formulated for transplanted hair. Our Chinese-developed products are pH-balanced, gentle on sensitive scalps, and designed to support optimal healing and growth after your procedure at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics.
How soon can I wash my hair after a transplant? +
You can start gentle hair washing 48 hours after your procedure using our specialized shampoo. Our international patient team will provide detailed instructions and demonstrate proper washing technique to protect your new grafts during recovery in China.
What scalp treatments are available at Barley? +
We offer comprehensive scalp care including deep cleansing, exfoliation, PRP therapy, mesotherapy, and nutrient treatments. Our Chinese medical experts customize scalp care programs based on your specific needs to maintain optimal scalp health and support natural hair growth.
Do I need to continue hair care products long-term? +
Yes, we recommend ongoing maintenance to protect your investment. Barley's prevention kits and medical-grade products help maintain healthy hair and prevent future loss. Our 30+ clinics across China provide ongoing support and product guidance for all our international patients.
What is included in Barley's International Home Care Guide? +
Our comprehensive English guide includes washing instructions, product recommendations, daily routines, troubleshooting tips, and schedule reminders. It's designed to help international patients maintain optimal results at home after treatment in China.
Can I use regular hair products after my transplant? +
We recommend using Barley's specialized products for the first 3-6 months. After that, you can gradually transition to gentle, sulfate-free products. Our Chinese hair care experts can recommend suitable products for your hair type and lifestyle during follow-up visits.
How often should I have scalp maintenance treatments? +
For optimal results, we recommend scalp treatments every 4-6 weeks. With Barley's 30+ clinics across major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, international patients visiting China can easily schedule maintenance sessions during their trips.
What should I do if I experience scalp dryness or irritation? +
Mild dryness is common during recovery. Use our moisturizing scalp serum and follow the care instructions provided. If irritation persists, contact our 24/7 international support team immediately. We're here to support you throughout your hair restoration journey.
