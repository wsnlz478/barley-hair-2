Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What areas can be treated with hair thickening? +
Hair thickening can address diffuse thinning, crown thinning, temple recession, and overall volume loss. Our expert doctors in China will assess your specific areas of concern and create a personalized plan to add density where you need it most.
How does microneedle technology improve hair thickening results? +
Our patented Chinese microneedle technology uses 0.6-1.0mm precision instruments that allow us to place follicles more densely without damaging existing hairs. This achieves higher density, more natural direction, and better survival rates compared to traditional methods.
Can hair thickening be done without cutting existing hair? +
Absolutely! Our no-shave hair thickening technique is perfect for adding volume while keeping your existing hair. We carefully place new follicles between your existing hairs, so no one will notice you had a procedure. It's ideal for professionals who want discreet treatment.
How many grafts are needed for hair thickening? +
Hair thickening typically requires 800-2,500 grafts depending on the area size and desired density. During your free consultation at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics, we'll evaluate your specific needs and provide an exact graft count recommendation.
Will the thickened hair look natural? +
Yes! Our Chinese medical team is trained in artistic placement techniques. We match hair direction, angle, and curl pattern exactly to your existing hair. The gradual density transition and natural placement ensure results that look completely authentic.
Can women get hair thickening treatment? +
Absolutely! Women make up a significant portion of our hair thickening patients. We understand the unique needs of female pattern hair loss and create softer, more natural-looking density enhancement that complements feminine hairlines and hairstyles.
How long does it take to see thickening results? +
Transplanted hairs typically shed in the first 2-3 weeks (this is normal!). New growth begins at 3-4 months, with visible thickening at 6 months. Full density results are typically seen at 9-12 months as the transplanted hairs mature.
Can I combine hair thickening with medical treatments? +
Yes! Many patients benefit from combining hair thickening transplantation with medical therapies (Minoxidil, Finasteride, PRP, laser therapy). This comprehensive approach stabilizes existing hair loss while adding new density for optimal, long-lasting results.
Is crown thickening more challenging than other areas? +
Crown thickening requires special attention to the natural whorl pattern and hair direction. Our expert doctors in China are specially trained in crown restoration techniques, creating natural-looking density that follows your existing growth pattern perfectly.
What is the recovery like after hair thickening? +
Recovery is minimal! Most patients return to work within 2-3 days. Any mild redness or swelling subsides in 5-7 days. Our nationwide network in China means you can have convenient follow-ups at any of our clinics near you.
