Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

Why should I choose hair restoration at Barley in China? +
Save 50-70% compared to US/UK/Australia prices while receiving world-class quality at Barley. We use patented microneedle technology, have 18+ years of experience, and provide comprehensive international patient support including airport pickup, translation services, and 24/7 assistance.
How long do I need to stay in China? +
Most international patients stay 3-7 days. The procedure is done in one day at Barley, and you can typically fly home 3-5 days after treatment. We'll help arrange your travel and accommodations to make your stay comfortable.
Is there a language barrier at Barley? +
Not at all! Barley has English-speaking doctors and staff available 24/7. We provide full translation services for all consultations, procedures, and aftercare instructions. You'll have a dedicated international patient coordinator throughout your journey.
What makes Barley's technology different? +
Barley's patented microneedle technology uses 0.6-1.0mm precision instruments for implantation, achieving minimal trauma, faster healing, higher density, and more natural results compared to traditional methods. Barley holds 10+ patents and is Sullivan-certified.
How soon will I see results? +
You'll begin seeing new hair growth at 3-4 months. Full results are typically visible at 9-12 months, with continued improvement up to 18 months. Barley provides ongoing remote follow-ups to monitor your progress after you return home.
Can women get hair transplants at Barley? +
Absolutely! Women make up a significant portion of Barley's patients. We offer specialized techniques for female pattern hair loss, eyebrow restoration, and other female-specific hair concerns at all our clinics across China.
Why is Barley considered a leader in hair transplantation? +
Barley has been pioneering hair restoration in China since 2006, with 18+ years of experience, 30+ clinics nationwide, 10+ technical patents, and Sullivan certification. Our patented microneedle technology and commitment to excellence make us one of the most trusted names in hair transplant globally.
What sets Barley apart from other hair transplant clinics? +
Barley's unique advantages include our proprietary microneedle implantation technology, extensive international patient experience, 24/7 English support, transparent pricing with 50-70% savings compared to Western countries, and the scale of 30+ clinics ensuring consistent quality across China.
What is the success rate of hair transplants at Barley? +
Barley achieves a 98% graft survival rate with our patented microneedle technology. This high success rate is due to our precision implantation techniques, experienced medical teams, and strict quality control standards across all 30+ of our clinics in China.
Can I combine treatments like FUE and PRP at Barley? +
Absolutely! Barley offers comprehensive treatment plans that can combine FUE hair transplant with PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy, scalp micropigmentation, or medical treatments like Minoxidil/Finasteride for optimal results. Our Chinese doctors will create a personalized plan just for you.
