Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What are the most common types of hair loss? +
The most common types include androgenetic alopecia (genetic hair loss), diffuse thinning, alopecia areata, postpartum hair loss, traction alopecia, and telogen effluvium. Our expert doctors in China will diagnose your specific condition and recommend the best treatment approach.
Can hair loss be reversed without surgery? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive non-surgical treatments including FDA-approved medications (Minoxidil and Finasteride), low-level laser therapy, PRP therapy, and follicle nutrition treatments. These can stop hair loss, stabilize shedding, and promote new hair growth in many cases.
How quickly can I see results from non-surgical hair loss treatment? +
Most patients begin to see stabilization of hair loss within 1-3 months. New hair growth typically becomes visible at 3-6 months, with optimal results achieved at 9-12 months. Our Chinese medical team provides regular monitoring throughout your treatment journey.
What is PRP therapy for hair loss? +
PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy uses your own blood plasma, enriched with growth factors, to stimulate dormant hair follicles and promote natural hair growth. It's a safe, natural treatment that works well alone or in combination with other therapies at our clinics across China.
Is postpartum hair loss permanent? +
Postpartum hair loss is usually temporary, occurring 2-6 months after childbirth due to hormonal changes. However, it can sometimes persist or reveal underlying genetic hair loss. We offer specialized treatments to help restore hair volume and confidence for new mothers.
Can women benefit from hair loss treatment? +
Absolutely! Women make up a significant portion of our hair loss patients. We offer specialized treatment plans designed specifically for female pattern hair loss, including non-surgical options that address the unique needs of women at all our clinics in China.
How do I know which treatment is right for me? +
During your free consultation at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics, our expert doctors will perform a comprehensive scalp analysis, discuss your medical history, and evaluate your specific hair loss pattern. We'll then create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs and goals.
What is the difference between medical therapy and hair transplantation? +
Medical therapy (medications, laser, PRP) works to stop hair loss and stimulate existing follicles. Hair transplantation is for areas that have already lost hair permanently, moving healthy follicles from the donor area. Our Chinese experts will recommend the best approach or combination for you.
Can I combine surgical and non-surgical treatments? +
Yes! Many patients benefit from a combination approach. Non-surgical treatments can stabilize hair loss and improve scalp health before transplantation, and continue to support growth after surgery. Our integrated approach at Barley ensures optimal, long-lasting results.
What should I do if I'm just starting to notice hair loss? +
The earlier you address hair loss, the better! Schedule a free consultation at any of our clinics in China. Early intervention with medical therapy can often stop progression and preserve existing hair. Waiting may limit your treatment options and require more extensive procedures later.
