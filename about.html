Barley: China's Hair Transplant Pioneer Since 2006

As China's first and largest specialized hair transplant hospital chain, Barley has led innovation in hair restoration for over 18 years. Founded in Beijing in 2006, Barley pioneered microneedle hair transplant technology across China and has been setting industry standards across Asia ever since.

Barley's mission is to bring world-class hair restoration to patients from China and around the world through our patented microneedle technology. With 30+ directly operated clinics spanning major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, Hangzhou, and Wuhan, Barley provides comprehensive one-stop solutions accessible to patients worldwide.

From our Beijing headquarters to our Shanghai flagship clinic, every Barley clinic in China maintains the same international standards of excellence, Sullivan-certified quality, and commitment to patient satisfaction that international patients trust and value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What makes Barley unique among hair transplant clinics in China? +
Barley is China's first and largest specialized hair transplant hospital chain, founded in 2006 with 18+ years of experience, 30+ clinics nationwide, 10+ patented technologies, 8x Sullivan certifications, and over 1 million successful procedures performed.
Why should international patients choose Barley for hair restoration in China? +
International patients choose Barley for our world-class quality at 50-70% savings compared to Western countries, comprehensive international patient services including airport pickup and translation, 24/7 English support, and our proven track record with thousands of international patients.
What is Barley's patented microneedle hair transplant technology? +
Barley's patented microneedle technology uses precision 0.6-1.0mm instruments for follicle implantation, achieving minimal trauma, faster healing, higher density, more natural results, and reduced post-operative discomfort compared to traditional hair transplant methods.
How many clinics does Barley operate across China? +
Barley operates 30+ directly managed clinics across major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, Chongqing, Hangzhou, Wuhan, Nanjing, and Xi'an, all maintaining the same international standards of excellence.
What certifications does Barley hold for international standards? +
Barley holds 8x Sullivan certifications, demonstrating our commitment to international standards of excellence, patient safety protocols, quality management systems, and clinical outcomes that international patients can trust when traveling to China for hair restoration.
How many hair transplant procedures has Barley successfully performed? +
Barley has performed over 1 million successful hair restoration procedures since our founding in 2006. This extensive experience means our medical team has seen and successfully treated every type of hair loss condition, making us the most trusted choice in China.
Is Barley's quality consistent across all clinic locations in China? +
Yes! Every Barley clinic maintains identical international standards, Sullivan-certified quality protocols, the same patented technologies, and our commitment to patient satisfaction. International patients receive the same premium care at any Barley location in China.
What international patient services does Barley provide? +
Barley provides comprehensive international patient services including airport pickup and transfer, full-time translation services, hotel arrangements near clinics, 24/7 English-speaking support, dedicated patient coordinators, and complete post-operative follow-up care.
What makes Barley's Chinese doctors qualified for international patients? +
Barley's doctors have extensive international patient experience, are trained in international standards, communicate effectively in English, and understand the aesthetic preferences and cultural expectations of international patients. Many have completed training or exchanges abroad.
Can I have remote consultations before traveling to China for my procedure? +
Absolutely! Barley offers free remote consultations via WeChat, WhatsApp, video call, or email. We can review your photos, discuss treatment options, provide personalized recommendations, and answer all your questions before you decide to travel to China for your hair restoration.
