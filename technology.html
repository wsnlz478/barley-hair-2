Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What makes Barley's technology unique in the hair transplant industry? +
Barley holds 10+ patented technologies including our revolutionary microneedle implantation system, 8x Sullivan certifications, and has been pioneering technological innovation in China since 2006 with 18+ years of experience.
Why is Barley's patented microneedle technology superior? +
Barley's microneedle technology uses precision 0.6-1.0mm patented instruments for follicle implantation, achieving minimal trauma, faster healing, higher density, more natural results, and reduced post-operative discomfort.
What hair transplant technologies does Barley offer? +
Barley offers FUE, FUT, no-shave hair transplant, robotic assistance, and our patented microneedle technology. Our doctors will recommend the best technology based on your individual needs and goals.
What is no-shave hair transplant technology? +
Barley's no-shave technology allows hair transplantation without shaving the donor area, making the procedure completely discreet. Perfect for professionals or anyone who prefers to keep their treatment private.
How does Barley's technology minimize trauma and speed recovery? +
Barley's patented microneedle technology creates minimal trauma during extraction and implantation, resulting in faster healing, less swelling and discomfort, and quicker return to normal activities compared to traditional methods.
What certifications does Barley hold for technology standards? +
Barley holds 8x Sullivan certifications, demonstrating our commitment to international standards of excellence, patient safety protocols, and quality management systems for all our technologies and procedures.
Can Barley's technology be used for both men and women? +
Yes! All of Barley's technologies including microneedle, FUE, FUT, and no-shave are suitable for both men and women. Our specialists will create personalized treatment plans for optimal results.
How many patented technologies does Barley have? +
Barley holds 10+ patented technologies covering extraction devices, implantation instruments, preservation solutions, and specialized tools that make our procedures safer, more precise, and more effective than traditional methods.
How does Barley ensure its technology stays at the forefront of innovation? +
Barley invests heavily in research and development, has a dedicated R&D team in China, collaborates with international experts, and continuously improves our technologies based on patient feedback and clinical outcomes.
Can international patients trust the safety of Barley's technologies? +
Absolutely! Barley's technologies are Sullivan-certified, backed by 10+ patents, and have been used in over 1 million successful procedures. Our commitment to patient safety is unmatched in China's hair transplant industry.
