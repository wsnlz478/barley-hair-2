Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What AI and machine learning technologies does Barley use? +
Barley is at the forefront of AI-assisted hair restoration in China. We use advanced AI for automated hair loss analysis, precision follicle placement guidance, and outcome prediction modeling. Our Chinese-developed AI systems help our doctors achieve more consistent, natural-looking results with every procedure at all 30+ clinics across China.
What advancements is Barley making in stem cell research? +
Our Chinese research team is actively exploring regenerative medicine approaches including follicle regeneration stimulation, cell-based therapy, and tissue engineering for hair restoration. While these are still in research phases, Barley remains committed to bringing cutting-edge technologies to our patients as they become available clinically.
How does robotic technology enhance hair transplant procedures? +
Barley uses robotic systems for enhanced precision in follicle extraction and automated assistance for faster, more consistent procedures. Our robotic technology works in conjunction with our Chinese medical experts and patented microneedle implantation to deliver exceptional results while maintaining the human touch and artistic judgment that only experienced doctors can provide.
Will future technologies make current hair transplant results obsolete? +
Not at all! Current hair transplant procedures using Barley's patented Chinese microneedle technology produce permanent, natural-looking results that will stand the test of time. Future technologies will complement, not replace, current procedures - offering additional options for maintenance, enhancement, and addressing different types of hair loss.
What is the future of unlimited donor hair supply? +
Researchers around the world, including our Chinese team at Barley, are exploring technologies that could potentially create unlimited donor hair supplies through tissue engineering and stem cell approaches. While promising, these technologies are still in development. For now, Barley's current procedures with our 30+ clinics across China provide excellent results with existing donor hair.
How does AI help with treatment planning? +
Our Chinese-developed AI systems analyze scalp condition, hair density, donor area quality, and patient goals to create optimized treatment plans. This assists our expert doctors in determining the optimal number of grafts, placement pattern, and technique selection for each individual patient at any of our Barley clinics in China.
Can future technologies help with existing transplanted hair? +
Yes! Future regenerative therapies could potentially enhance the quality and thickness of both transplanted and existing natural hair. Barley's Chinese research team is actively exploring these possibilities to provide our patients with ongoing options for maintaining and enhancing their results for years to come.
How does Barley stay at the forefront of hair restoration technology? +
With 18+ years of experience in China, 10+ Chinese patents, and active research partnerships, Barley continuously invests in advancing hair restoration technology. Our 30+ clinics across major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou ensure our patients always have access to the latest proven advancements while we carefully research future technologies.
