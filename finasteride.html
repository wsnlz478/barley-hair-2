Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How does finasteride work to stop hair loss? +
Finasteride works by inhibiting the 5-alpha-reductase enzyme, which converts testosterone to DHT (dihydrotestosterone) - the primary hormone responsible for male pattern baldness. By reducing DHT levels by up to 70%, finasteride prevents further miniaturization of hair follicles and allows existing hairs to recover and grow thicker. Barley's Chinese doctors can prescribe and monitor finasteride for appropriate male patients at all 30+ clinics across China.
Is finasteride suitable for women? +
No, finasteride is strictly for men and not approved for use by women, especially those who are pregnant or may become pregnant, due to potential risks to fetal development. Barley's Chinese medical experts offer safe, effective alternatives for women including minoxidil, PRP therapy, and specialized treatments at our clinics in Beijing, Shanghai, and other major Chinese cities.
What is the standard dosage of finasteride for hair loss? +
The standard dosage for hair loss is 1mg taken once daily, with or without food. Consistent daily use is important to maintain results. Barley's Chinese doctors will evaluate your specific condition and provide personalized recommendations during your consultation at any of our 30+ clinics across China.
How soon will I see results from finasteride? +
Most men begin to see results within 3-6 months, with optimal results typically visible at 9-12 months. Clinical studies show that 83% of men show improvement, 66% experience visible regrowth, and 90% see stabilization of hair loss. Barley's Chinese medical team provides ongoing monitoring to track your progress.
What are the potential side effects of finasteride? +
Finasteride is generally well-tolerated. Less than 2% of users experience rare sexual side effects, which are usually reversible upon discontinuation. Barley's Chinese doctors will discuss all potential risks and benefits with you during your consultation and monitor you throughout treatment to ensure your safety and comfort.
Can I use finasteride with a hair transplant? +
Absolutely! In fact, finasteride and hair transplantation work synergistically as the "gold standard" combination approach. Finasteride protects existing native hairs from further loss while transplanted hairs grow permanently. This combination maximizes and maintains your results. Barley's Chinese experts can create a personalized combined treatment plan for you.
What happens if I stop taking finasteride? +
If you stop taking finasteride, the DHT-inhibiting effects gradually wear off. Any hairs that were preserved or regrown due to finasteride may be lost over time, typically within 6-12 months. The transplanted hairs from your hair transplant will remain permanently as they're genetically resistant to DHT. Barley's Chinese doctors can help you understand the long-term commitment required.
Do I need a prescription for finasteride? +
Yes, finasteride is a prescription medication that must be prescribed and monitored by a qualified physician. Barley's expert Chinese doctors at all 30+ clinics across China can evaluate your suitability for finasteride, provide a prescription if appropriate, and monitor your progress throughout treatment with regular check-ups.
