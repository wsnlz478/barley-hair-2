Usage Guidelines

International standard application methods and dosage

Application & Dosage

Proper usage is essential for optimal results:

  • Strength Options: 2% for women, 5% for men (both available)
  • Application: Apply 1mL twice daily to dry scalp
  • Coverage: Focus on thinning areas and hairline
  • Timing: Allow 4 hours to dry before washing or bed

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Minoxidil

How long does it take to see results with Minoxidil? +
Most people begin to see visible results with Minoxidil after 3-4 months of consistent daily use. Maximum results typically appear at 9-12 months. It's important to use it every day as directed - stopping will cause any gained hair to shed within 3-4 months. Barley's Chinese doctors provide personalized guidance on usage throughout your treatment.
What's the difference between 2% and 5% Minoxidil? +
The 5% concentration is generally more effective for men, while 2% is the standard starting point for women to minimize potential side effects. Some women may use 5% off-label with medical supervision. At Barley, our Chinese hair restoration specialists will recommend the optimal strength based on your individual condition and goals at any of our 30+ clinics across China.
Can I use Minoxidil with a hair transplant? +
Absolutely! This is a powerful combination. Many patients use Minoxidil before transplant to strengthen existing hair, and after transplant to help maintain results and encourage thicker growth. Barley's doctors typically recommend waiting 2-4 weeks after surgery before resuming Minoxidil application to the transplant area to protect the new grafts.
Is the initial shedding with Minoxidil normal? +
Yes! Temporary increased shedding in the first 2-6 weeks is completely normal and actually a good sign. This means the treatment is working by pushing out old hairs to make way for new, healthier growth. Don't be discouraged - this phase passes quickly. Barley's team reassures patients about this normal process at all our clinics in China.
How does Barley recommend applying Minoxidil for best results? +
Apply 1mL of Minoxidil twice daily to completely dry scalp, focusing on thinning areas and the hairline. Allow at least 4 hours to dry completely before washing your hair or going to bed. Barley's Chinese medical experts demonstrate proper application technique during consultations to ensure you get optimal results from your treatment.
What makes Barley's Minoxidil guidance different? +
With 18+ years of experience in China, Barley's experts have treated tens of thousands of patients with Minoxidil. We don't just sell you a product - we provide personalized guidance, monitor your progress, and adjust your treatment plan as needed. Our 30+ clinics across China mean you can get consistent, expert care wherever you are in the country.
What happens if I stop using Minoxidil? +
If you stop using Minoxidil, the beneficial effects gradually wear off. Any hairs that were preserved or regrown due to Minoxidil will typically shed within 3-4 months. However, transplanted hairs from a hair transplant will remain permanently as they're genetically resistant to DHT. Barley's doctors can help you understand the long-term commitment required.
Can Minoxidil help with a receding hairline? +
Minoxidil works best for crown thinning, but can help some people with early receding hairlines. For more advanced hairline recession, hair transplantation is typically more effective. Barley's Chinese specialists can assess your specific pattern and recommend whether Minoxidil alone or combined with transplant would be best for you during your free consultation.
