Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How is the eyebrow design personalized? +
Our artistic team analyzes your face shape, eye shape, existing brows, and personal style to create a custom design. We consider facial symmetry, proportions, and your preferences to design eyebrows that frame your eyes beautifully and complement your features.
Where does the hair for eyebrow transplantation come from? +
Eyebrow hair is typically taken from the back of the scalp (donor area) where hair is finer and matches eyebrow texture best. Our Chinese doctors carefully select hairs with the right texture and curl pattern to create natural-looking brows.
How many grafts are needed for eyebrow restoration? +
Eyebrow transplantation typically requires 100-300 grafts per eyebrow depending on the desired fullness and existing hair. During your free consultation at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics, we'll assess your specific needs and provide an exact graft count.
Will transplanted eyebrows need trimming? +
Yes! Since the hair comes from the scalp, it will continue to grow like scalp hair. You'll need to trim your transplanted eyebrows every 1-2 weeks to maintain your desired shape and length. This becomes part of your regular beauty routine.
Can I choose my eyebrow shape? +
Absolutely! We work with you to understand your preferences - whether you want bold, natural, arched, or straight brows. Our artistic team will present several design options based on your facial analysis, and together we'll select the perfect eyebrow shape for you.
How long does an eyebrow transplant procedure take? +
Eyebrow transplantation typically takes 2-4 hours depending on the number of grafts needed. It's a comfortable outpatient procedure performed under local anesthesia at any of our clinics across China.
When will I see full eyebrow results? +
Transplanted hairs typically shed in the first 2-3 weeks (this is normal!). New growth begins at 3-4 months, and you'll see your eyebrows filling in nicely by 6 months. Full, mature results are typically visible at 9-12 months.
Can eyebrow transplantation fix scars or gaps? +
Absolutely! We can fill in gaps from over-plucking, scars from injuries or surgery, and naturally sparse areas. Our Chinese medical team specializes in scar camouflage and creating natural-looking brows even in challenging areas.
Is eyebrow transplantation painful? +
You'll receive local anesthesia to numb the area, so you won't feel pain during the procedure. There may be mild swelling or tenderness afterward that can be managed with over-the-counter medication. Most patients describe the experience as very comfortable.
What is the recovery like after eyebrow transplant? +
Recovery is quick! You can return to normal activities within 1-2 days. Any mild redness or swelling subsides in 3-5 days. Our team will provide comprehensive aftercare instructions to ensure optimal healing and results.
