Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers about robotic hair transplant technology

What is robotic hair transplant and how does it work at Barley? +
Robotic hair transplant uses advanced automated systems for follicle extraction and AI-assisted planning. At Barley in China, our robotic technology ensures unparalleled precision, consistent quality, and optimal graft survival rates compared to manual techniques.
Is robotic hair transplant more precise than manual extraction? +
Yes! Robotic systems can extract follicles with sub-millimeter precision, minimizing trauma and maximizing graft viability. Barley's robotic technology in China achieves 98%+ graft survival rates while maintaining our 50-70% cost advantage over Western clinics.
How long does a robotic hair transplant procedure take? +
Robotic procedures at Barley typically take 4-6 hours depending on graft count, with automated extraction significantly reducing procedure time while maintaining precision. All our 30+ Chinese clinics offer state-of-the-art robotic capabilities.
What are the benefits of AI-assisted hair transplant planning? +
AI planning at Barley analyzes your scalp, hair density, and loss pattern to create optimal transplant designs. Our Chinese-developed AI algorithms ensure natural-looking hairlines, perfect graft distribution, and personalized treatment plans for every patient.
Is robotic hair transplant available at all Barley clinics in China? +
Yes! Robotic hair transplant technology is available at our major clinics in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and other key Chinese cities. International patients can choose any of our 30+ locations for this advanced treatment.
Does robotic hair transplant hurt? +
No, you'll receive local anesthesia just like with manual procedures. The robotic system at Barley is extremely gentle, and many patients report even less discomfort than with traditional methods. Our Chinese medical team ensures your complete comfort throughout.
Can robotic systems handle large transplant cases? +
Absolutely! Barley's robotic technology excels at both small and large volume procedures. The automated systems can efficiently extract 3000+ grafts in a single session with consistent precision, making it ideal for extensive baldness cases.
What is the recovery like after robotic hair transplant? +
Recovery is similar to manual FUE but often faster due to minimal trauma. Most patients at Barley return to work within 2-3 days, with complete healing in 7-10 days. Our nationwide Chinese clinic network means convenient follow-ups anywhere.
