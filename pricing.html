Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers about pricing and costs

How is hair transplant pricing calculated at Barley? +
Pricing at Barley in China is based on the number of grafts needed, with no hidden fees. Our transparent pricing structure means you pay only for what you need, with quotes provided during free consultation at any of our 30+ Chinese clinics.
Why are hair transplants cheaper in China than in Western countries? +
Lower operational costs in China allow us to offer world-class hair restoration at 50-70% less than US, UK, or Australian clinics. Barley maintains the same international quality standards while passing significant savings to our international patients.
What is the average cost range for a hair transplant at Barley? +
Most procedures at Barley cost between $3,000-$8,000 USD depending on graft count and technique chosen. Compare this to $15,000-$25,000 in the US, and you'll see why international patients travel to China for our exceptional value.
Do different techniques have different pricing? +
Yes! Standard FUE is most affordable, micro-needle FUE offers premium results at mid-range pricing, no-shave FUE commands a slight premium for discretion, and FUT offers maximum value for large cases. We'll help you choose the best option during consultation.
Is the consultation free? +
Absolutely! All consultations at Barley are completely free with no obligation. We'll assess your needs, create a personalized treatment plan, and provide a transparent quote - all before you make any commitment at any of our Chinese clinics.
Are there any hidden fees? +
No hidden fees at Barley! Your quote includes everything - consultation, procedure, medications, and follow-up care. We believe in complete transparency, especially for our international patients traveling to China from abroad.
Do you offer payment plans or financing? +
Yes! Barley offers flexible payment options for qualified patients. During your consultation at any of our Chinese clinics, we can discuss available financing plans to make your hair restoration journey more manageable.
Is your pricing transparent with no hidden fees? +
Absolutely! We believe in complete pricing transparency. During your free consultation, you'll receive a detailed, all-inclusive quote covering everything from the procedure to post-op care. There are no surprise charges or hidden fees at any of our Chinese clinics.
