Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

Where can I see real before and after hair transplant photos in China? +
Barley's website showcases authentic before and after hair transplant photos from real patients. Browse our success cases gallery to see transformations for hairline, baldness, women's hair, beard, and eyebrow restoration, all performed at our clinics in China.
How can I trust hair transplant before and after photos? +
Barley only displays genuine before and after photos from patients who have given consent. Our success cases show consistent, natural results achieved with our 18+ years of expertise and patented technologies, giving you confidence in the quality of work we deliver.
What should I look for in hair transplant success cases? +
When evaluating success cases, look for natural hairline design, appropriate density, correct angle and direction of growth, and overall aesthetic harmony. Barley's cases demonstrate all these qualities, showing why we're a trusted choice for hair restoration.
Can I see FUE hair transplant before and after results? +
Yes! Barley has extensive FUE hair transplant before and after photos in our success cases gallery. See how our advanced FUE technique with microneedle technology delivers scar-free extraction and natural-looking results for patients worldwide.
Are there women's hair transplant before and after photos available? +
Absolutely! Barley specializes in women's hair restoration and our success cases include many female patient transformations. Browse our gallery to see before and after photos for women's hairline restoration, density improvement, and other female-specific hair loss solutions.
How soon can I expect to see before and after-like results? +
Most patients begin seeing new hair growth at 3-4 months, with final results typically visible at 12-18 months. Your timeline will be similar to the success cases you see that match your procedure type and hair loss situation.
What makes Barley's before and after photos different from others? +
Barley's before and after photos stand out because of our exceptional naturalness, artistic hairline design, and consistent density across all cases. With 1 million+ procedures performed since 2006, our success rate and expertise are unmatched in the industry.
Can I see beard transplant before and after results too? +
Yes! Barley offers comprehensive facial hair restoration including beard and eyebrow transplants. Our success cases gallery features before and after photos for these procedures, showing how we can enhance facial aesthetics with natural-looking results.
Why should I choose Barley based on their success cases? +
Barley's success cases demonstrate our commitment to excellence with 30+ clinics across China, Sullivan-certified quality, 10+ patents, and 18+ years of experience. Our before and after photos show consistent, outstanding results that international patients trust.
Will I get the same results as Barley's success cases show? +
During your free consultation, our doctors will evaluate your individual situation including donor hair quality, hair loss pattern, and personal goals. While results vary by individual, we'll show you similar success cases and discuss realistic expectations for your specific situation at Barley.
