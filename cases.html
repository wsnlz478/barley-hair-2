Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What makes Barley's success cases so impressive and authentic? +
Barley has performed over 1 million successful procedures with 30+ clinics since 2006. Our success cases show real patient transformations demonstrating consistent, natural-looking results achieved with our patented technologies and expert doctors.
Why are Barley's before and after results trusted worldwide? +
Barley's success cases show authentic patient transformations with 18+ years of expertise, Sullivan-certified quality, and proven results that international patients can trust when considering hair restoration in China.
Can I see success cases similar to my situation at Barley? +
Absolutely! During your consultation, we can show you success cases similar to your hair loss pattern, hair type, and desired results to help you visualize what can be achieved for you personally.
What types of success cases does Barley have? +
Barley has success cases for every type of hair restoration including hairline transplantation, baldness restoration, crown thickening, eyebrow transplantation, beard transplantation, and women's hair restoration.
How soon will I see results like Barley's success cases? +
Most patients begin seeing new hair growth at 3-4 months, with final results typically visible at 12-18 months. Your timeline will be similar to the success cases you see that match your procedure type.
Are Barley's success cases from real patients? +
Yes! All success cases show real Barley patients who have given permission to share their transformations. These authentic results demonstrate what our doctors and technologies can achieve.
Can Barley achieve results like the success cases I see? +
During your free consultation, our doctors will evaluate your individual situation and discuss realistic expectations based on your donor hair quality, hair loss pattern, and goals compared to similar success cases.
What's the success rate for results like Barley's cases? +
Barley achieves excellent success rates with our patented technologies and expert medical team. Our 98%+ success rate and 1 million+ procedures demonstrate consistent, outstanding results you can trust.
How do Barley's success cases compare to other clinics worldwide? +
Barley's success cases show exceptional naturalness and density, often exceeding results from Western clinics while being 50-70% more affordable. Our 18+ years of expertise and 10+ patents set us apart globally.
Will my results be included in Barley's success cases? +
With your permission, we may include your transformation in our success cases to help other patients. However, this is completely optional and your privacy is always our priority.
