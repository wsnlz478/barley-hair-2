Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What beard styles can be created? +
We can create any style including full beards, goatees, mustaches, sideburns, goatees with mustaches, soul patches, and more. Our artistic team will work with you to design a style that matches your facial features, lifestyle, and personal preferences.
Where does the hair for beard transplantation come from? +
Beard hair is typically taken from the back of the scalp (donor area) where hair is thick and durable. In some cases, we can use existing beard hair as donor if available. Our Chinese doctors carefully select hairs with the right texture for facial hair.
How many grafts are needed for a beard? +
Beard transplantation typically requires 500-2,500+ grafts depending on the style and desired fullness. A mustache might need 300-600 grafts, while a full beard could need 1,500-3,000 grafts. During your consultation, we'll provide an exact graft count for your desired style.
Will transplanted beard hair need trimming? +
Yes! Since the hair comes from the scalp, it will continue to grow like scalp hair. You'll need to trim and shape your transplanted beard regularly to maintain your desired style. This becomes part of your regular grooming routine.
Can I choose my beard style? +
Absolutely! We work with you to understand your preferences - whether you want a rugged full beard, a sharp goatee, a classic mustache, or something in between. Our artistic team will present design options and help you select the perfect style for your face.
How long does a beard transplant procedure take? +
Beard transplantation typically takes 3-6 hours depending on the number of grafts needed. It's a comfortable outpatient procedure performed under local anesthesia at any of our clinics across China.
When will I see full beard results? +
Transplanted hairs typically shed in the first 2-3 weeks (this is normal!). New growth begins at 3-4 months, and you'll see your beard filling in nicely by 6 months. Full, mature results are typically visible at 9-12 months.
Can beard transplantation fix scars or patchy areas? +
Absolutely! We can fill in gaps from genetics, scars from injuries or surgery, and naturally patchy areas. Our Chinese medical team specializes in scar camouflage and creating natural-looking beard coverage even in challenging areas.
Is beard transplantation painful? +
You'll receive local anesthesia to numb the area, so you won't feel pain during the procedure. There may be mild swelling, redness, or tenderness afterward that can be managed with over-the-counter medication. Most patients describe the experience as very comfortable.
What is the recovery like after beard transplant? +
Recovery is quick! You can return to normal activities within 1-2 days. Any mild redness or swelling subsides in 3-7 days. You'll need to be gentle with the area while it heals, and we'll provide comprehensive aftercare instructions.
